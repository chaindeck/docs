Welcome to the Blockchain Data Portal. This platform provides access to blockchain data related to the evm network, specifically focusing on transaction data from the genesis block. Blockchain technology, known for its decentralized and immutable ledger, has numerous applications, from financial transactions to supply chain management. This data is essential for researchers, developers, and analysts interested in studying evm transaction's history.

The data is sourced from the Ethereum mainnet via a reliable global archive node network, ensuring its credibility and authenticity. Its primary purpose is to support research and analysis of Ethereum transactions. This data is updated in real-time, offering an up-to-date perspective on the network.
